Welcome to the Natural Language Processing (NLP) Lab at Virginia Tech!

About us

The NLP Lab at VT is mainly working on the fields of Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. We are particularly interested in building efficient models and benchmarks that can encourage machines to perform human-level intelligence, especially for NLP applications such as information extraction, machine reading comprehension and natural language generation.
